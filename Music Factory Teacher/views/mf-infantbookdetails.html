Play Welcome Song

Welcome Song

Objective of this lesson

CD track:

Vol. 2 Track #19 of It's Time to Sing Hello

Activity Procedure

Activity Variations:
  • Encourage children to sing and respond following actions written in [*brackets]
  • Substitute "clap our hands" and "stomp your feet" with any other actions such as:
    • Pat on different facial features, such as touch your nose, touch your eyebrows, touch your cheeks
    • Use locomotive movements such as Twirl to the right, twirl to the left, march to the right, march to the left, jump to the right, jump to the left
    • Personalise the song by using a child’s name to sing on "Hello child’s name" instead of "Hello children" and have the child respond back by singing individually
Audio 1: It's Time to say Hello
Play Goodbye Song

Goodbye Song

Objective of this lesson

CD track:

Vol. 2 Track #19 of It’s Time to Sing Goodbye

Activity Procedure

Activity Variations:
  • Encourage children to sing and respond following actions written in [*brackets]
  • Substitute "wave your hands" and "sing goodbye" to other farewell actions such as:
    • Wave goodbye, Sing farewell
    • Hug a friend, Say/Sing goodbye
    • Give high five, So long friend
    • Personalise the song by using a child’s name to sing on "Goodbye child’s name" instead of "Goodbye children" and have the child respond back by singing individually
Audio 1: It's Time to sing Goodbye
